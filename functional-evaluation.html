Network Survey Vehicle

The existing Road asset inventory location and condition can be assessed by using a Network Survey Vehicle (NSV). These vehicles offer extensive capabilities and can extract a wide variety of information from all types of pavements across road networks and airports.

The survey equipment will have sufficient power to carry the pavement data collection and this includes customizable modules like

  • Camera – calibrated imagery enables to measure, take Geo-reference make it best to extract Roadside inventory

  • DGPS based submeter accurate Geo references,

  • GIPSITRAC – Road Geometry

  • Distance – more accurate Distance measurements,

  • Digital Laser profiler – can deliver IRI, Rut, Texture

  • Automatic Crack Detection (ACD) system – can detects the Crack, surface defects using latest AI trained model.

This equipment possesses the capability of safely surveying across all network terrains, on any survey speed acceptance criteria and temperature conditions. These features enable to collect – a good quality data in very short duration, resulting in a safer work environment. The data collection and processing are fully transparent and do not allow the data to be tampered with in any way.

For very large networks, an ACD-equipped vehicle can be used to automatically identify and quantify cracking using Automatic Crack Detection software, which is faster than manual visual rating. The Vehicle equipped with 13LP-equipped can operate both on unsealed roads and Sealed roads, where vehicle fitted with ACD vehicles are restricted to sealed roads.
